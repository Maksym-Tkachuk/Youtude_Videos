Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how our application ("Service") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our video publishing tool.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate the Service:

  • Authentication tokens: OAuth tokens from YouTube and TikTok are stored locally on your device to enable video publishing. These tokens are never transmitted to any server other than the respective platform APIs.
  • Video content: Videos you create are processed locally on your device and uploaded directly to your social media accounts.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information
  • We do not collect email addresses
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • We do not store your videos on our servers

4. How We Use Information

Authentication tokens are used solely to publish content to your connected social media accounts (YouTube, TikTok) on your behalf. No data is used for advertising, profiling, or any other purpose.

5. Data Storage

All data, including OAuth tokens and application settings, is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store user data. Tokens are saved in your local configuration directory and can be deleted at any time.

6. Third-Party Services

The Service connects to the following third-party APIs:

  • YouTube Data API v3 — for uploading videos and posting comments. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • TikTok Content Posting API — for publishing videos. Subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties. Data is only transmitted to YouTube and TikTok APIs as necessary to fulfill your publishing requests.

8. Data Retention

Authentication tokens remain on your device until you manually delete them. We do not retain any copies of your data.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your stored authentication tokens at any time
  • Revoke the application's access through YouTube or TikTok account settings
  • Request information about what data the Service accesses

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
